Studio Flottant de Louis Vuitton
Conceptual Architecture
Studio Flottant de Louis Vuitton is a visionary architectural masterpiece that merges luxury fashion with modern design. The house features a cube-like glass structure suspended delicately, anchored by a robust concrete frame, reminiscent of the way large bells are hung. The transparent glass walls provide a floating, weightless effect, allowing natural light to flood the space. The exterior is adorned with iconic Louis Vuitton embroidery prints, seamlessly integrating the brand’s signature patterns into the architecture. This elegant floating studio stands as a fusion of art, fashion, and modern structural ingenuity.

La MAISON ASSYMETRIQUE
Residential Architecture
La Maison Asymétrique is a strikingly unconventional white house that defies traditional architecture. Characterized by its sharp, asymmetric design, the structure avoids typical 90-degree angles, instead embracing bold 135 and 45-degree forms throughout. This angular design gives the house a dynamic, almost sculptural appearance. Repeated geometric patterns accentuate its uniqueness, creating a rhythmic visual flow that contrasts with its irregular shape. The house’s minimalist white facade enhances the boldness of its asymmetry, making it an architectural statement that challenges the norms of residential design.

BOYS QUOTE-EYES I
Small-Scale Architecture
Boys Quote-Eyes I is a striking black-and-white themed house, originally conceptualized as a simple boys' quarters but elevated into a modern architectural gem that draws attention with its bold beauty. The sleek, minimalist design features sharp contrasts between black and white surfaces, creating a refined, eye-catching aesthetic. Its spacious balcony, adorned with lush plants, extends the living space outdoors, while greenery surrounds the house, adding a fresh, natural touch. The design's unexpected elegance and modern sophistication make it impossible to overlook—truly a house that “quotes eyes.”

Small-Scale Architecture
Boys Quote-Eyes II is a two-tone beige masterpiece that continues the tradition of elegance and attention to detail. CASA del DESIERTO
Residential Architecture
CASA del DESIERTO is a stunning one-story home set against the vast desert landscape, designed to harmonize with its surroundings while standing out with modern elegance. The reflective beige facades mirror the natural hues of the desert, creating a subtle, ever-changing interaction with the environment. Architectural forms—sharp lines and bold angles—are sculpted around the house, adding depth and movement to the design. Large openings and expansive windows invite light into the interior, fostering a seamless connection between the indoors and the expansive desert outdoors. CASA del TROPICA
Residential Architecture
CASA del TROPICA is a refined single-story residential home, designed to embody both elegance and subtle personalization. Its beige-colored facade exudes warmth and sophistication, while sculptural architectural elements wrap around the structure, giving it a distinct character. One of the most unique features is its balcony and exterior shapes, which, from a specific angle, subtly reveal the letter “P”—a nod to the house’s owner, Peter. This creative integration makes CASA del TROPICA not only a modern tropical retreat but also a personalized architectural statement that merges style with identity.

WHITESTONE RETREAT
Residential Architecture
This small residential house combines a timeless white facade with bold black accents for a modern yet classic appearance. The porch and two large frontal columns, which support a balcony adorned with plants, feature elegant brick facades, adding texture and warmth to the design. The open garage stands out with a series of repeated concrete structures surrounding it. These structures are evenly spaced and vary in height—rising towards the center and tapering down again—creating a rhythmic, sculptural effect.

GREENLINE HEALTH FACILITY I
Hospital Architecture
This modern clinic is designed with a harmonious blend of functionality and aesthetic appeal. The exterior features beige and white facades, creating a calming atmosphere for patients. At the heart of the building is a prominent glass facade that provides transparency and openness, flanked by entrances on both sides. On either side of the central structure, equal-sized rooms mirror each other, maintaining symmetry in the design. The materials and aesthetic of the central glass feature contrast beautifully with the more traditional elements of the side rooms, emphasizing the clinic's modernity. Within the central structure, a striking white sculptural concrete feature serves as a focal point, while intricate line-like green accents evoke the visual rhythm of hospital monitors, symbolizing health and vitality.

GREENLINE HEALTH FACILITY II
Hospital Architecture
This modern clinic presents a striking architectural evolution, featuring an innovative design that enhances its functional appeal while maintaining an inviting atmosphere. The outer sides of the building are adorned with warm brick facades, creating a welcoming contrast to the white interiors. The inner facades are asymmetrically divided into two sections, with the farther parts larger than the sections closest to the central structure, adding visual interest and depth. The central structure has shifted to a more dominant white aesthetic, with a reduced emphasis on glass to enhance privacy and comfort. A unique sculptural roof structure crowns the clinic, showcasing protruding angular beams that add a dynamic element to the design. This roof not only serves an architectural purpose but also contributes to the building's distinctive silhouette. The central feature remains a striking representation of health and vitality, with green line accents symbolizing the rhythms of hospital monitors, seamlessly integrating nature with modern healthcare.
